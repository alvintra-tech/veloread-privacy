# VeloRead Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

VeloRead is designed as a **local-first reading and thinking tool**.  
Your documents, highlights, insights, and knowledge data remain on **your device** unless you explicitly export or share them.

This Privacy Policy explains how VeloRead handles information.

---

# 1. Information We Do Not Collect

VeloRead does **not require an account** and does **not collect personal information** such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Contacts  
- Location data  
- Personal reading content  

Your documents, highlights, insights, and notes remain **stored locally on your device**.

---

# 2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored **only on your device**:

- PDF documents you open  
- Highlights and annotations  
- Insights and notes  
- Reading briefs  
- Knowledge graph connections  
- Application preferences and settings  

This information is **not transmitted to external servers by VeloRead**.

---

# 3. Subscriptions and Payments

VeloRead offers an optional subscription called **VeloRead Plus**.

VeloRead Plus may include features such as:

- Removal of advertisements  
- Unlimited exports  
- Access to premium features  

All payments are processed securely through **Google Play Billing**.

VeloRead **does not collect or store payment information**.

Subscriptions:

- Automatically renew unless cancelled  
- Can be managed through **Google Play account settings**  
- May vary by region and currency  

You may cancel your subscription at any time through the **Google Play Store**.

---

# 4. Subscription Verification (RevenueCat)

VeloRead uses **RevenueCat**, a subscription management platform, to validate purchases made through Google Play.

RevenueCat may process limited technical information necessary for subscription verification, including:

- Anonymous app user identifier  
- Subscription entitlement status  
- Purchase transaction status  
- Device/platform metadata required for billing validation  

RevenueCat **does not receive access to your reading data**, including:

- PDF documents  
- Highlights  
- Insights  
- Reading notes  
- Knowledge graph data  

More information:  
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

---

# 5. Advertisements

The free version of VeloRead may display advertisements using **Google AdMob**.

AdMob may collect or process limited technical information such as:

- Advertising identifiers  
- Device information  
- Interaction with advertisements  

This information is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Users who subscribe to **VeloRead Plus** will have advertisements removed.

---

# 6. Data Sharing

VeloRead **does not sell or share personal data**.

Limited technical information may be processed by third-party services strictly for:

- Subscription verification (RevenueCat)  
- Payment processing (Google Play Billing)  
- Advertisement delivery (Google AdMob)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

---

# 7. Data Security

Because VeloRead is a **local-first application**, your reading data remains stored on your device.

Users are responsible for protecting their device and maintaining backups of important documents.

---

# 8. Children's Privacy

VeloRead is not directed toward children under the age of **13**.

The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

# 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

The most recent version will always include the **latest revision date**.

<div class="policy-shell">
  <h1>VeloRead Privacy Policy</h1>
  <div class="policy-meta">Last updated: March 2026</div>

  <div class="policy-note">
    VeloRead is designed as a <strong>local-first</strong> reading and thinking tool. Your documents, highlights, insights, and knowledge data remain on your device unless you explicitly export or share them.
  </div>

  ## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

  VeloRead does **not require an account** and does not collect personal information such as:

  - Name
  - Email address
  - Phone number
  - Contacts
  - Location data
  - Personal reading content

  Your documents, highlights, insights, and notes are stored **locally on your device**.

  ## 2. Data Stored on Your Device

  The following information is stored **only on your device**:

  - PDF documents you open
  - Highlights and annotations
  - Insights and notes
  - Reading briefs
  - Knowledge graph connections
  - Application preferences and settings

  This information is **not transmitted to external servers** by VeloRead.

  ## 3. Subscriptions and Payments

  VeloRead offers an optional subscription called **VeloRead Plus** which may include:

  - Removal of advertisements
  - Unlimited exports
  - Access to future premium features

  All payments are processed securely through **Google Play Billing**.

  VeloRead does **not collect or store payment information**.

  Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled through your **Google Play account settings**.

  You may manage or cancel your subscription at any time in the Google Play Store.

  ## 4. Subscription Verification (RevenueCat)

  VeloRead uses **RevenueCat**, a subscription management platform, to validate purchases made through Google Play.

  RevenueCat may process limited technical information required for billing verification, such as:

  - Anonymous app user identifier
  - Subscription entitlement status
  - Purchase transaction status
  - Device/platform metadata required for billing validation

  RevenueCat **does not receive**:

  - PDF documents
  - Highlights
  - Insights
  - Reading notes
  - Knowledge graph data

  More information:  
  https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

  ## 5. Advertisements

  The free version of VeloRead may display advertisements using **Google AdMob**.

  AdMob may process limited technical information including:

  - Advertising identifiers
  - Device information
  - Interaction with ads

  This information is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy:  
  https://policies.google.com/privacy

  Users who subscribe to **VeloRead Plus** will have advertisements removed.

  ## 6. Data Sharing

  VeloRead does **not sell or share personal data**.

  Limited technical information may be processed by third-party services strictly for:

  - Subscription verification (RevenueCat)
  - Payment processing (Google Play Billing)
  - Advertisement delivery (Google AdMob)

  These services operate under their own privacy policies.

  ## 7. Data Security

  Because VeloRead operates as a **local-first application**, your reading data remains on your device.

  You are responsible for the security of your device and backups.

  ## 8. Children's Privacy

  VeloRead is not directed toward children under 13.

  The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  ## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

  The most recent version will always include the **latest revision date**.

  ## 10. Contact

  Developer: **Alvintra**  
  Application: **VeloRead**

  If you have questions regarding this policy, please contact the developer through the app listing on the Google Play Store.
